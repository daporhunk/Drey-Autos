Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a test drive?

+

You can schedule a test drive by calling us at +1 (800) 555-1234, emailing sales@dreyautos.com, or using the contact form above. Most test drives can be arranged within 48 hours. We also offer virtual walkarounds via video call.

What is included in the vehicle inspection?

+

Our comprehensive inspections include mechanical evaluation, exterior/interior condition assessment, paint depth measurement, service history review, and a detailed report with photographs. Each vehicle is independently verified for authenticity and mechanical soundness.

Do you offer financing options?

+

Yes, we offer flexible financing options through our partners. Contact our sales team to discuss rates, terms, and qualification requirements. We work with buyers to find solutions that fit their needs.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

+

Absolutely! We accept trade-ins on most vehicles. Submit photos and details of your current car, and our appraisers will provide a quote. Trade-in value can be applied toward your new vehicle purchase.

What warranty do you provide?

+

We offer extended warranty options on most vehicles. Details vary by car age and mileage. All vehicles come with a 7-day inspection period where you can return the vehicle if not completely satisfied.
